Research Interests

Theoretical Particle Physics — BSM Phenomenology. My research focuses on the phenomenology of physics beyond the Standard Model, with emphasis on extra-dimensional scenarios and their cosmological and astrophysical signatures. A central theme is the Kaluza-Klein (KK) gravitational sector: its spin-2, vector, and scalar towers, the sum rules and symmetry structures governing their interactions, and their interplay with dark matter, axions, and neutrinos across collider, astrophysical, and cosmological scales.
